--- --- In the big picture of modern software engineering, Chocolatey bridges the gap between manual software management and fully automated infrastructure as code (IaC) practices. Its simplicity, compatibility with CI/CD pipelines, and scalability across both desktops and servers make it a key enabler for DevOps, LowCode adoption, and hybrid environments. As an open and extensible tool, it integrates seamlessly with platforms like Intune, Azure, and MyGet for enterprise use.
